About Me

Hello All, I am Krishna Chaitanya Bavana but most people know me as Krish Bavana. I am currently a first year undergraduate student majoring in Computer Science (and I am planning to Minor in Entrepreneurship) at University of Illinois at Chicago (UIC) and my expected graduation is May, 2023. I attended Naperville North High School for four years of High School and Graduated in May, 2019. I love writing and expressing my views on Technologies, Leadership, Time-management and my Journey. I have always loved writing from my childhood. Even though I started blogs in July, 2019, I always wrote something from a very early age. After four years of High School, I found my passion in Computer Science, Data Science and Entrepreneurship. Even though I have seen myself as a full time writer, I always wanted to write about Technology and Leadership. I always loved challenging myself in every phase of my life. I wanted to try something new that no else would. I did both Computer Science Classes and Yearbook (Journalism) Classes together during my high school life. Now I am planning to Blogging, Computer Science, Data Science and learn about Entrepreneurship together. I update my blogs every week here and on UIC Radio Blogs.
